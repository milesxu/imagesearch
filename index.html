Image Search Abstraction Layer

You can search images at https://image-search-milesxu.herokuapp.com/api/imagesearch, for example:

https://image-search-milesxu.herokuapp.com/api/imagesearch/natural%20landscape?offset=10

the parameter offset is optional.

You can get your search history at https://image-search-milesxu.herokuapp.com/api/history.
